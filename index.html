We started this for ourselves

Two people, both 59, both determined to feel better without giving up the food we love. Mediterranean cuisine. West African flavors. Cajun and Creole. Soul Food. BBQ. All adapted to strict keto — under 20g net carbs daily.

We've lost 20 lbs each and kept it off. Our friends started asking what we were doing. This site is the answer.
