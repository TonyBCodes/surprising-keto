How it started

The Surprising Keto Journey began as a personal experiment to keep the bold, layered flavors of African, Mediterranean, Cajun, and Soul Food cooking while cutting carbs to under 20g a day.

What we cook

Every recipe on this site is built around real ingredients you can find at any grocery store or ethnic market. No specialty keto products required.

The results

Down 20 lbs and counting. Net carbs under 20g daily. Over 160 recipes published across more than 20 cuisine traditions.
